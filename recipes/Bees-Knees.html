Bee's Knees

Description

The Bee's Knees is a classic Prohibition-era cocktail known for its bright, refreshing flavor. Made with gin, fresh lemon juice, and honey syrup, it offers a smooth balance of tart and sweet. The honey adds a floral depth that softens the botanical notes of the gin, making it an easy, elegant drink thatâ€™s both timeless and approachable.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 3/4 oz Honey Syrup
  • 3/4 oz Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake the combination
  3. Strain into a coupe glass
