Gimlet

Description

The Gimlet is a crisp, no-frills cocktail traditionally made with gin and fresh lime juice, often sweetened with a touch of simple syrup. Known for its sharp, citrusy bite and clean finish, the Gimlet is a refreshing, balanced drink that has stood the test of time.
